The Problem

Africa's greatest asset is
invisible to the system.

600 million young Africans have skills, drive and work history — but no way to prove it. Every time they approach a new employer, a bank, or a government programme, they start from zero. AYL fixes that permanently.

01
Youth Have No Credit File
Training certificates, completed gigs, positive supervisor ratings — none of it travels. Banks cannot lend to someone with no financial history. Employers cannot trust someone with no verified track record.
02
Employers Screen in the Dark
CVs are fiction. Dropout rates in BPO, logistics and retail exceed 40% in the first 90 days. Security recruitment creates dangerous crowds with no screening. Every hire is a gamble.
03
Government Cannot Measure Impact
Billions spent on youth training programmes with no way to know if anyone got a job. No shared database. No outcome tracking. No evidence base for policy.
The Solution

One identity. One score.
Permanent record.

AYL gives every young person a verified Agentics Youth ID and a continuously updated Employability Score — built from real behavioural data, visible to every employer, portable across borders, and improving with every gig, course and positive interaction.
