Integration with National Programmes

Built to work with what already exists

NYEP
National Youth Employment Programme
Youth enrolled in NYEP can be matched to verified private sector opportunities via AYL. Every placement is tracked, every retention measured. NYEP outcome dashboards available to Ministry of Employment in real time.
NABIA
National Buffer Stock & Food Security Authority
NABIA youth agri-workers registered on AYL gain a portable identity and employability record — making seasonal agricultural labour part of a long-term career trajectory rather than invisible casual work.
GES
Ghana Education Service / TVET Programmes
AYL connects to all registered TVET providers — every course completion flows directly into the student's AYL record and Employability Score. GES can view aggregate graduate employment outcomes by institution for the first time.
GPS
Ghana Police Service / Security Agencies
Digital pre-screening, controlled batch invitations and a complete digital audit trail for every public service recruitment intake. Eliminates dangerous crowding. Creates defensible accountability for selection decisions.
GhCard
National ID Authority — Ghana Card
AYL anchors every Youth ID to the Ghana Card — no parallel database, no duplicate registration, no identity fraud. AYL extends NIA coverage into the informal youth economy without adding burden to NIA systems.
